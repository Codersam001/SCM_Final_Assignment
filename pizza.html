CHEESY PIZZA

Ingredients

For the Pizza Dough:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water (about 110째F/45째C)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

For the Pizza Sauce:

  • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

For the Toppings:

  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup pepperoni slices (optional)
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers (optional)
  • 1/4 cup sliced onions (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)
  • Olive oil (for brushing the crust)

Instructions

  1. Make the Pizza Dough:
    In a small bowl, dissolve the sugar in the warm water and add the yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until it becomes foamy.
    In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir until the dough begins to come together.
  2. Knead the Dough:
    Turn the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 8-10 minutes, or until the dough is smooth and elastic. If the dough is sticky, add a little more flour as needed.
    Form the dough into a ball, place it in a lightly oiled bowl, and cover with a damp towel. Let it rise in a warm place for 1-1.5 hours, or until it doubles in size.
  3. Prepare the Sauce:
    In a small saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic powder and oregano and cook for 1 minute. Add the tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer on low heat for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Preheat the Oven:
    Preheat your oven to 475째F (245째C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up.
  5. Shape the Dough:
    Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release the air. Divide the dough into two portions if you want two smaller pizzas, or keep it whole for one large pizza.
    Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness (about 12-14 inches in diameter).
  6. Assemble the Pizza:
    Transfer the rolled-out dough to a baking sheet or a preheated pizza stone.
    Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce over the dough. Sprinkle with shredded mozzarella cheese and add your favorite toppings.
  7. Bake the Pizza:
    Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  8. Serve:
    Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh basil if desired, and enjoy!

Tips:

  • Thin vs Thick Crust: For a thinner crust, roll the dough out more thinly. For a thicker crust, let the dough rise for a longer time and roll it out slightly thicker.
  • Crust Flavor: You can brush the crust with a bit of garlic butter or olive oil before baking for extra flavor.
  • Custom Toppings: Add your favorite toppings like mushrooms, olives, sausage, or pineapple. The possibilities are endless!
  • Pizza Stone: If you have a pizza stone, use it for a crispier crust. Preheat the stone in the oven for at least 30 minutes before baking.
