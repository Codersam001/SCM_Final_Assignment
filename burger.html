YUMMY HOMEMADE CLASSIC BURGER

Ingredients

For the Burger Patties:

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef (80% lean, 20% fat)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Freshly ground black pepper (to taste)
  • Optional: 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Optional: 1 teaspoon garlic powder or onion powder

For the Burger Assembly:

  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce (optional)
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Cheese slices (American, cheddar, Swiss, etc.)
  • Ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Patties:
    In a mixing bowl, combine the ground beef with salt, pepper, and any additional seasonings or Worcestershire sauce. Gently mix the beef without overworking it to keep it tender. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and form into round patties, making them slightly larger than the buns. Press a small indentation in the center of each patty to prevent puffing up.
  2. Cook the Patties:
    Preheat a grill or stovetop griddle over medium-high heat. Place the patties on the grill or pan. Cook for about 3-5 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 160°F (71°C) for well-done. Add cheese slices during the last minute of cooking to melt over the patties, if desired.
  3. Toast the Buns:
    Cut the hamburger buns in half and toast them lightly on the grill or in a skillet until golden brown.
  4. Assemble the Burgers:
    Place the cooked patty on the bottom half of each toasted bun. Add lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles, if desired. Spread your favorite condiments (ketchup, mustard, mayo) on the top bun. Top with the other half of the toasted bun.
  5. Serve and Enjoy:
    Serve immediately with a side of fries, chips, or a fresh salad.

Tips:

  • For Juicy Burgers: Avoid pressing down on the patties while cooking, as this releases juices and can make the burger dry.
  • Seasoning Variations: You can add chopped onions, garlic, or other herbs and spices to the burger mix for extra flavor.
  • Cheese Options: Experiment with different cheeses, such as cheddar, blue cheese, or pepper jack, for a unique twist.
