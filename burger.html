Classic Homemade Burger

Ingredients

Burger Patties:

  • 1 lb ground beef (80% lean)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce (optional)

For Assembly:

  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 cheese slices
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion
  • Condiments: ketchup, mustard, mayo

Instructions

  1. Make patties: Mix beef with seasonings. Form into 4 patties, ¾-inch thick.
  2. Cook: Grill or pan-fry 3-4 minutes per side (160°F internal temp).
  3. Add cheese: Place cheese on patties last minute to melt.
  4. Toast buns: Lightly toast cut sides.
  5. Assemble: Build burgers with your favorite toppings.

Tips:

  • Don't overwork the meat - keeps burgers tender
  • Make a thumbprint in center to prevent bulging
  • Let burgers rest 2 minutes before serving

Nutrition Info

Per serving (1 burger):

Nutrient Amount
Calories 450
Protein 30g
Fat 25g
Carbs 30g
