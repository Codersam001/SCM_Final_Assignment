Delicious Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

For The Cake:

  • 1¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1½ cups granulated sugar
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1½ tsp baking powder
  • 1½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

For The Frosting:

  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3 cups powdered sugar
  • ¼ cup whole milk
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. Mix dry ingredients: Whisk together flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add wet ingredients: Beat in eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla until smooth.
  4. Add boiling water: Carefully mix in boiling water (batter will be thin).
  5. Bake: Divide batter between pans. Bake 30-35 minutes until toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Cool: Let cool in pans 10 minutes, then transfer to wire racks.
  7. Make frosting: Beat butter until creamy. Alternately add cocoa-sugar mixture and milk with vanilla and salt.
  8. Frost cake: Once completely cooled, frost between layers and over top and sides.
  9. Serve: Enjoy! Store leftovers covered at room temperature.

Tips:

  • Fluffy frosting: Add milk 1 tbsp at a time if too thick
  • Decoration: Top with chocolate shavings or berries
  • Extra moist: Substitute ¼ cup oil with sour cream
  • Make ahead: Bake layers a day early, frost before serving
