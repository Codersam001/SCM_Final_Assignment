DELICIOUS CANNOLI

Ingredients

For the shells:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine (or as needed)
  • Oil for frying

For the filling:

  • 15 oz ricotta cheese (drained)
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips (optional)
  • Zest of 1 orange (optional)

For Garnishing:

  • Powdered sugar (for dusting)
  • Chopped pistachios (optional, for rolling the edges)
  • More chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Make the Shells: In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder (if using), cinnamon, and salt. Add the cold butter and rub it into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  2. Stir in the beaten egg and wine. Mix until the dough comes together. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5 minutes, or until smooth. Cover with plastic wrap and let it rest for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Shape the Shells: Roll out the dough on a floured surface to about 1/8-inch thickness. Use a round cookie cutter or a glass to cut out circles about 4 inches in diameter. Wrap each circle of dough around a cannoli tube, sealing the edges with a little egg white.
  4. Fry the Shells: Heat the oil in a deep pan to 350°F (175°C). Fry the shells for 2-3 minutes until golden brown. Remove and set aside to drain on paper towels. Once cool, slide the shells off the tubes.
  5. Prepare the Filling: In a medium bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy. Stir in the mini chocolate chips and candied orange peel (if using).
  6. Fill the Shells: Pipe or spoon the ricotta filling into the cooled cannoli shells. Don’t fill too early to avoid sogginess.
  7. Serve: Dust with powdered sugar and serve immediately for the best crispness and flavor.

Tips for Perfect Cannoli

  • Drain the Ricotta: Ricotta can be watery, so drain it well using a cheesecloth or fine mesh sieve before mixing.
  • Rest the Dough: Let the dough rest for at least 30 minutes to make rolling it easier.
  • Frying Temperature: Maintain the oil at 350°F (175°C) for crisp, golden shells.
  • Fill Just Before Serving: Fill the shells only before serving to keep them crispy.
  • Pistachio Garnish: Roll the ends of the cannoli in chopped pistachios for extra flavor and color.
