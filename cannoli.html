Delicious Cannoli

Ingredients

For the shells:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine (or as needed)
  • Oil for frying

For the filling:

  • 15 oz ricotta cheese (drained)
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips (optional)
  • Zest of 1 orange (optional)

For garnishing:

  • Powdered sugar (for dusting)
  • Chopped pistachios (optional)
  • Extra chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions

  1. Make the Shells: In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, cocoa powder (if using), cinnamon, and salt. Add butter and rub it in until crumbly.
  2. Add the egg and wine. Mix to form a dough. Knead on a floured surface for 5 minutes. Cover and rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Shape: Roll out and cut into 4-inch circles. Wrap around cannoli tubes, sealing with egg white.
  4. Fry: Heat oil to 350°F (175°C). Fry shells 2–3 mins until golden. Drain and cool.
  5. Filling: Mix ricotta, powdered sugar, vanilla. Add chips and orange zest if desired.
  6. Assemble: Fill cooled shells just before serving to prevent sogginess.
  7. Serve: Dust with powdered sugar. Garnish as desired.

Tips for Perfect Cannoli

  • Drain Ricotta: Use cheesecloth or a fine mesh sieve.
  • Rest Dough: It makes rolling easier.
  • Monitor Frying Temperature: Keep it steady at 350°F.
  • Fill at the Last Minute: Keeps the shell crisp.
  • Add Pistachios: For color and crunch.
