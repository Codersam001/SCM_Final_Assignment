CHICKEN

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon (juiced and zested)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp dried rosemary
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, garlic, thyme, rosemary, salt, pepper, and paprika. Stir well to combine.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag or shallow dish and pour the marinade over them. Seal and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 4 hours for more flavor.
  3. Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Once hot, place the chicken on the grill.
  4. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes per side, or until fully cooked (internal temperature should reach 165째F or 75째C).
  5. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing.
  6. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley, if desired, and serve with your favorite side dishes.

Cooking Tips

  • Resting the chicken: Let the chicken rest after grilling to keep it juicy and tender.
  • Marinate the chicken: For even more flavor, marinate the chicken in the olive oil mixture for at least 30 minutes before grilling.
  • Temperature check: Use a meat thermometer to ensure the chicken reaches 165째F (75째C) for safety and tenderness.
  • Customize the seasoning: Feel free to experiment with different spices like cumin, chili powder, or oregano for varied flavor.
  • Grill marks: For those perfect grill marks, avoid moving the chicken too much while grilling!
